# Product Metrics

### North Star Metric

**Total Annual Savings Identified ($)**: This aggregates the "value" SpendPilot provides to the ecosystem and directly correlates with the amount of potential credit volume Credex can capture.

### Input Metrics

1.  **Consultation CTA Click-Rate**: Measures the "hunger" for Credex’s primary service.
2.  **Audit Completion Rate**: Total clicks on "Get Audit" vs. Form Submissions (Targets friction in the UI).
3.  **Viral Coefficient (K-factor)**: Number of new audits generated per shareable URL visit.

### Pivot Trigger

If the **Conversion to Consultation** falls below 2% for users with >$500 in savings, it indicates that users don't trust the audit results or the transition to Credex feels too "salesy." We would pivot to a "Self-Service Credit Portal" instead of a consultation booking.
