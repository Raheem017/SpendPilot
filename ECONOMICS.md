# Unit Economics & Revenue Impact

### Lead Value for Credex

A "converted lead" is a startup that moves from retail AI pricing to Credex-negotiated credits.

- **Avg. Monthly Spend**: $10,000.
- **Credex Margin**: ~10% via bulk-purchase arbitrage.
- **Annual Lead Value**: $12,000 ARR in gross margin per customer.

### Funnel Math

- **Audit Completed**: 1,000 users.
- **"High Savings" Identified (>$500/mo)**: 200 users (20%).
- **Consultation Booked**: 20 users (10% of high-savings cases).
- **Closed Sale**: 4 users (20% close rate).
- **Total Value**: 4 \* $12,000 = **$48,000 ARR generated from 1,000 audits**.

### CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

- **Initial**: $0 (Organic/Viral).
- **Scaled (LinkedIn Ads)**: $100 per "Audit Completed." With a 0.4% total conversion rate to sale, CAC per customer = $25,000. While high, the LTV of a multi-year credit contract makes this profitable by Month 24.

### The Path to $1M ARR (18 Months)

To drive $1M ARR, we need ~84 active enterprise customers.

- **Requirement**: This tool must process ~21,000 audits.
- **Strategy**: Focus on the "Unfair Channel" (VC partnerships) to keep CAC low and lead quality high.
